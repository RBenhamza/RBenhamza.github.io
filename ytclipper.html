Why creating the youtube clipper ?

I've seen many youtube clippers online but all of them were either missing something, either too long/too complex to use or needed an account. I wanted to make a simple clipper with just the 3 needed infos

How does it work ?

The clipper uses the pytube library to download the video from youtube, and the moviepy library cuts with the given timecodes.
